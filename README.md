# SavePlate
SavePlate is a platform that facilitates the connection between local food establishments (restaurants, bakeries, and more) and charities based in the community, allowing them to fight against food waste in urban areas. We enable companies to convert their daily surplus into social good and reduce disposal fees, and we enable food banks to access fresh, high-quality meals for the hungry. Charities find and coordinate themselves around real-time donation leads and can grow their operational capacity through our platform. Companies receive simplified sustainability reporting, automated monitoring of tax incentives, and deeper connections with the community through increased brand loyalty.
